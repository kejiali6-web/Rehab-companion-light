Problem Observation Diary

  • Slow, fragile progress: Rehabilitation is step-by-step. Patients often lose motivation after small setbacks.
  • Privacy vs. care: Caregivers struggle to balance assistance with preserving patient privacy during toileting and dressing.
  • Lost voice: Patients with speech loss can't express needs, causing emotional isolation and unmet requirements.
  • Social isolation: Reduced mobility limits activity and social contact, harming long-term recovery motivation.
